### Booking Engine Performance
The speed and user experience of your booking engine directly impacts conversion rates. Look for solutions that load quickly and offer a streamlined checkout process.

### Payment Processing
Secure payment gateways with support for multiple currencies and payment methods are essential for international guests.

### Channel Management
Integration with major OTAs and metasearch engines helps maintain rate parity and maximize distribution.

## Making the Right Choice

Consider these factors when selecting reservation software:

1. **Property Size**: Ensure the solution scales with your needs
2. **Budget**: Compare total cost of ownership including setup and monthly fees
3. **Integration**: Verify compatibility with your existing systems
4. **Support**: Evaluate the quality and availability of customer support
5. **Features**: Match features to your specific operational requirements
